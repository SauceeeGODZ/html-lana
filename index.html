About Me

Singer & Artist

Lana Del Rey is an American singer, songwriter, and record producer. Born Elizabeth Woolridge Grant in New York City in 1985, she adopted her stage name in 2005 and first gained attention in 2011 with her single "Video Games". Del Rey's music style is often characterized as "Hollywood sadcore" due to her nostalgic and melancholic lyrics, and her music often features a mix of vintage pop, rock, and hip-hop elements. She has released several critically acclaimed albums, including "Born to Die" (2012), "Ultraviolence" (2014), and "Lust for Life" (2017). Del Rey has been recognized for her distinctive voice and artistic style, as well as her contributions to music and popular culture.
